Frequently asked questions

How do I book a session?+
Browse services, choose a professional and a time that suits you, then confirm. You'll get instant confirmation and reminders — no phone calls needed.
Are the professionals verified?+
Yes. Every Serene pro passes identity and background checks, certification reviews and a hands-on quality trial before they ever appear on the platform.
Can I get a session at home?+
Absolutely. Most services are available both at home and in studio. Your pro arrives fully equipped, so you can simply relax in your own space.
What is your cancellation policy?+
You can reschedule or cancel free of charge up to 4 hours before your appointment. Anything later may incur a small fee that goes to your professional.
How does payment work?+
Pay securely in-app with transparent, upfront pricing — no surprises. Every booking is backed by our happiness guarantee, or we'll make it right.
